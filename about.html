Get to know us

CLEAN IT Cleaning Services is a millennial homegrown Malaysian company that offering and seeks to revolutionize the way we book part-time cleaners. As we know, most of the people nowadays are busy with their career commitment so they don’t have enough time to ensure their house in a good condition and stay clean. It seems a great opportunity to become one of the helpers that clean their house. At the end of the day, we as one of the biggest cleaning company in future always to kick back, have fun and make sure the customers satisfied.
